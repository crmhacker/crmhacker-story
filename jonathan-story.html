Founder Story

From cooking for friends to cooking up revenue systems.

I’m Jonathan — builder of CRM Hacker. Former quota crusher, lifelong tinkerer, weekend chef. I help teams turn scattered tools into a predictable pipeline.

Chapter 1 — The Maker

Before dashboards and deal desks, it was knives and cast-iron. Cooking taught me the craft of flow: mise en place, timing, feedback loops. That rhythm turned into a mindset — simplify, stage, execute.

“Great meals and great funnels look the same on the inside: everything in the right place, at the right time.”

Chapter 2 — The Closer

In sales, I learned how tiny frictions kill momentum. Wrong fields, missing data, slow handoffs. I started fixing what annoyed me — automations, sequences, cleaner opp stages — and numbers jumped. So did my obsession.

“If reps have to fight the CRM, you won’t like the forecast.”

Chapter 3 — The Builder

CRM Hacker was born from a simple promise: zero fluff, all signal. Clean data, tight processes, and automations that feel invisible. Less juggling; more selling.
