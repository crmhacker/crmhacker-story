Jonathan’s Journey

From kitchens and quotas to building CRM Hacker — the path that shaped my approach to systems, sales, and growth.

Chapter 1 — The Maker

Before revenue ops, my life was in the kitchen. Cooking taught me discipline, flow, and the importance of clean prep — principles that later became the backbone of how I design systems.

Chapter 2 — The Closer

In corporate sales I saw how messy systems crushed momentum. I started fixing what annoyed me — automations, cleaner opp stages, faster routing — and suddenly numbers jumped. That’s when my passion for RevOps clicked.

Chapter 3 — The Builder

CRM Hacker was born to strip away noise and make tools disappear into the flow of selling. Today, I help tech firms build Salesforce setups that speed up deals, clean up data, and give leaders clarity.

What I Do Now

Salesforce revops end-to-end: architecture, migrations, automations, analytics, enablement. I design and ship, then prove lift with numbers.
